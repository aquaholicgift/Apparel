    What printing techniques are used for custom sock printing?

    The most common printing techniques include dye sublimation, direct-to-garment (DTG) printing, and screen printing. Dye sublimation is great for detailed and colorful designs, DTG allows for high-quality prints directly on the socks, and screen printing is ideal for larger orders with fewer colors.

    What makes personalized socks effective for marketing and promotional activities?

    Personalized socks are effective for marketing due to their practicality and visibility. They can be designed with company logos, slogans, or specific color schemes, creating a memorable and unique promotional item. Socks are also worn frequently, ensuring repeated exposure of your brand.

    What materials are used for printed socks?

    Common materials for printed socks include cotton, wool, polyester, and blends. The choice of material depends on the desired comfort, durability, and printing needs. For promotional purposes, a blend of cotton and synthetic fibers is often preferred for a balance of comfort, durability, and ease of printing.
